# Micro Teaching Lesson Plan V2: Virtual Mission with Space Guide alFA

**Subject:** IPAS / Space Science  
**Topic:** Solar System Exploration (Explorasi Tata Surya)  
**Target:** Grade 6-7 Students  
**Duration:** 5 - 7 Minutes  
**Format:** Google Meet (Virtual, 1-on-1)  
**Character:** alFA - Space Guide at Angkasa Explorer Station  (Android Life-form Assistant)
**Media:** Solar System Explorer Web App (Screen Share)

---

## 🎯 Learning Objectives
1. Cadet can identify planet order in the Solar System
2. Cadet can compare basic planetary characteristics
3. Cadet actively engages with digital learning media

---

## 🎬 Lesson Flow & Script

### 1. Opening (1 Minute)
**Skill Assessment:** *Keterampilan Membuka Pelajaran*

**(Screen: Camera on, friendly posture. Background can be space-themed if available)**

**alFA:** "Ah, signal received! Good morning, Cadet. Connection status... stable. Selamat datang di Angkasa Explorer Station. I'm alFA, your personal Space Guide today."

**alFA:** "Before we start our mission, quick check - can you see my screen clearly? Bagus. Now, kita akan melakukan virtual tour ke solar system kita. Sounds exciting?"

**(Pause for response - acknowledge with a nod)**

**alFA:** "Perfect. Sebelum kita launch, let me activate our motto. Repeat after me: 'Explore, Discover, Understand!' Coba?"

**(Wait for cadet to repeat, give positive feedback)**

**alFA:** "Excellent energy! Oke Cadet, mission briefing dimulai. I'm sharing my screen now... standby."

**(Action: Share screen showing Solar System Explorer homepage/Explore page)**

---

### 2. Core Content / Explanation & Media (3 Minutes)
**Skill Assessment:** *Keterampilan Menjelaskan & Menggunakan Media*

**(Screen: Navigate to Solar System Map view)**

**alFA:** "Alright, di layar kamu bisa lihat our stellar neighborhood. In the center, we have the Sun - Matahari kita. Tanpa si bola api ini, everything would freeze dalam hitungan detik."

**alFA:** "Now, let's take a journey. Starting from the closest planet..."

**(Action: Click/Zoom to Mercury)**

**alFA:** "First stop: Mercury. Planet terkecil dan paling dekat dengan Matahari. Permukaannya penuh kawah karena sering dibombardir meteor. No atmosphere untuk melindunginya."

**(Action: Navigate to Venus)**

**alFA:** "Next, Venus. Many people think Mercury is hottest karena paling dekat, tapi actually... Venus holds the record. Kenapa? Because it has super thick atmosphere - CO₂ yang tebal menciptakan greenhouse effect ekstrim. Temperature bisa reach 470°C!"

**(Action: Zoom to Earth-Mars region, highlight Goldilocks Zone if available)**

**alFA:** "Sekarang kita masuk comfort zone - literally. Ini yang disebut **Goldilocks Zone**. Not too hot, not too cold. Perfect untuk liquid water. Dan di sini ada kita - Earth, dan tetangga kita, Mars."

**alFA:** "Mars ini menarik, Cadet. It's cold, tapi suhunya paling mirip Bumi dibanding planet lain. Makanya banyak scientists interested untuk future colonization."

**(Action: Jump to Jupiter - show size comparison if feature exists)**

**alFA:** "Now, hold on tight. We're jumping to the giant - Jupiter! Lihat size-nya compared to Earth..."

**(Show visual or describe)**

**alFA:** "Jupiter's diameter is approximately 11 times Earth's diameter. Bayangkan, kamu bisa line up 11 Bumi dari ujung ke ujung Jupiter. It's a gas giant - no solid surface. Dan fun fact: Jupiter acts like our protector, menarik asteroids dengan gravitasinya before they reach us."

---

### 3. Interaction & Engagement (2 Minutes)
**Skill Assessment:** *Keterampilan Berinteraksi*

**alFA:** "Oke Cadet, sekarang alFA needs your help. Ada beberapa data yang perlu verification. Ready?"

---

**Question 1:**

**alFA:** "Based on our tour tadi, which planet has the temperature paling mirip dengan Bumi? Is it Jupiter, Venus, or Mars?"

**(Scenario A - Correct Answer: Mars)**

**Cadet:** "Mars?"

**alFA:** "Correct! Exactly right. Mars has temperatures yang relatif moderate compared to others. Kamu pay attention dengan baik!"

**(Scenario B - Wrong Answer: Venus)**

**Cadet:** "Venus?"

**alFA:** "Hmm, kurang tepat, Cadet. Venus is actually extremely hot - 470 derajat, remember? Yang paling dekat suhunya dengan Bumi adalah **Mars**. Mars itu cold, tapi range-nya lebih manageable, around -60°C average. Venus terlalu extreme."

---

**Question 2:**

**alFA:** "Next question. Tadi kita bilang Jupiter diameternya 11 kali Bumi. Kalau gitu, Jupiter is a... rocky planet atau gas giant?"

**(Scenario A - Correct Answer: Gas Giant)**

**Cadet:** "Gas giant?"

**alFA:** "Absolutely! You're right. No solid ground di Jupiter, semuanya gas dan liquid. Great job!"

**(Scenario B - Wrong Answer: Rocky planet)**

**Cadet:** "Rocky planet?"

**alFA:** "Kurang tepat ya, Cadet. Rocky planets itu yang kayak Bumi - ada permukaan padat. Tapi Jupiter itu **gas giant**, composed mostly of hydrogen dan helium. Kalau kamu try to land di sana, you'd just keep falling through the gas. Interesting, kan?"

---

**Question 3:**

**alFA:** "Last verification. Why is Earth special? Kenapa Bumi bisa support life while others can't? Any idea?"

**(Scenario A - Correct/Close Answer: mentions water, temperature, atmosphere)**

**Cadet:** "Because it has water? / In Goldilocks Zone? / Right temperature?"

**alFA:** "Excellent thinking! Yes, exactly - Earth berada di Goldilocks Zone, so temperature is right untuk liquid water, plus we have protective atmosphere. Perfect combination!"

**(Scenario B - Vague/Incomplete Answer)**

**Cadet:** "Because... it's good?"

**alFA:** "Well, you're not wrong, haha. But more specifically, Earth ada di **Goldilocks Zone** - jarak yang pas dari Matahari. Not too hot, not too cold. Jadi air bisa exist dalam bentuk liquid, dan kita punya atmosphere untuk protection. That's what makes it 'good' for life. Make sense?"

---

### 4. Closing (1 Minute)
**Skill Assessment:** *Keterampilan Menutup Pembelajaran*

**alFA:** "Alright Cadet, mission accomplished. Data verification complete!"

**alFA:** "Let me summarize apa yang kita explore today: Kita mulai dari Mercury yang kecil dan panas, Venus dengan greenhouse effect-nya, Earth di Goldilocks Zone, Mars our future home candidate, and Jupiter the giant protector. Each planet punya unique role dalam solar system kita."

**alFA:** "Remember: Space exploration itu bukan hanya tentang technology, tapi juga about understanding our place in the universe."

**alFA:** "For your next mission - I recommend kamu explore the app sendiri. Try the Quiz Mission feature untuk test knowledge-mu lebih dalam. Deal?"

**(Wait for acknowledgment)**

**alFA:** "Perfect. Thank you for joining today's expedition, Cadet. Keep exploring, keep learning. alFA signing off. See you in the next orbit!"

**(Action: Wave goodbye, friendly smile before ending screen share)**

## ✅ Assessment Criteria Checklist

| Aspek | Implementation |
|:------|:---------------|
| **Membuka Pelajaran** | Greeting + connection check, motto ice breaker ("Explore, Discover, Understand"), mission briefing intro |
| **Menjelaskan** | Explained planetary facts (Mercury craters, Venus greenhouse effect, Goldilocks Zone concept, Jupiter as protector) with bilingual narrative |
| **Media Pembelajaran** | Screen share of Solar System Explorer app, navigating between planets, using visual comparisons |
| **Berinteraksi** | 3 verification questions with prepared responses for both correct & incorrect answers, gentle correction technique ("kurang tepat, lebih tepatnya...") |
| **Menutup Pelajaran** | Summary of key points, encouragement for independent exploration, friendly sign-off |

---

## 📝 Notes for Execution

**Virtual Adaptation:**
- Maintain good eye contact with camera (not just screen)
- Use cursor/pointer to highlight elements on screen
- Speak clearly and pause for cadet's response
- Smile and nod to maintain engagement

**Gentle Correction Framework:**
- Never say "Salah" / "Wrong"
- Use "Kurang tepat" / "Hmm, close but not quite"
- Follow with "Lebih tepatnya..." / "Actually..."
- Re-explain the concept briefly
- End with validation "Make sense?" / "Understand now?"

**Bilingual Balance:**
- Use English for key scientific terms (gas giant, atmosphere, Goldilocks Zone)
- Use Indonesian for explanations and transitions
- Keep code-switching natural, not forced
- Ensure comprehension by mixing both languages smoothly
